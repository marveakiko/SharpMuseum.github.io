1925

Japan's First Crystal Radio

This is Japan's first Crystal Radio.

Following the Great Kanto Earthquake of 1923, Tokuji restarted business in Osaka, where he saw a future in the field of radio.

In April 1925, he and his associates made history as they succeeded in assembling Japan's very first crystal radio.

The sound quality was good but the volume was low, so people had to use headphones to listen to the radio.
